# Japan Phrasebook 🇯🇵

An offline, installable PWA of the ~200 most useful things a friendly Australian
traveller wants in Japan — polite phrases, tap-to-hear pronunciation, and
sign-reading practice.

- **Install:** open the site on your phone → iPhone: Share → *Add to Home Screen*;
  Android: ⋮ → *Install app*. Open it once and it works fully offline afterwards.
- **Voice:** uses your device's built-in Japanese voice (offline). Pick a clearer
  voice from the dropdown at the top.
- **Tech:** plain HTML/CSS/JS, no build step, no dependencies. `sw.js` caches
  everything for offline use; `make_icons.py` regenerates the app icons.
